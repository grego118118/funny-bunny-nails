Our Story

A little salon with a lot of heart.

Funny Bunny Nails & Spa is Northampton's neighborhood nail destination — a place where you can unwind, treat yourself, and leave feeling your best.

Walk-ins are always welcome, and appointments are available for those who like to plan ahead.
